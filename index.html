❓ Arda's Sculks – Developer Q&A

How did the idea for this mod come about?
It all started with a suggestion from a friend. They told me “you should make a mod that expands the Deep Dark theme”, and I got to work. That idea eventually turned into Arda's Sculks.
What tools did you use?
I used MCreator throughout development. It allowed me to prototype quickly and still shape the codebase exactly how I needed it.
What was the biggest technical challenge?
Without a doubt, coding the Shadow Hunter's mechanics. Writing abilities like teleportation and fast kicks took weeks. On top of that, it was my first time designing a dedicated boss arena, so solving the lag issues that appeared was another huge hurdle.
Which loader does the mod work best with?
The most stable and compatible version is NeoForge. It also runs on Forge, but I developed and tested primarily on NeoForge, so that's where the best experience is.
What does the mod actually add to the game?
Arda's Sculks expands the sculk and Deep Dark theme in almost every direction. New mechanics, armor, weapons, boss fights, decoration blocks, and even farming areas – my goal was to deepen this theme as much as possible.
Who is the most notable boss in the mod?
Shadow Hunter. A fast‑kicking, teleporting boss that keeps you on edge the entire fight. It forces you to think strategically instead of just clicking mindlessly.
What kind of player is this mod for?
Adventure seekers, hands down. If you're looking for an end‑game challenge, enjoy intense boss encounters, and want to dive deeper into the mystery of the Deep Dark, this mod is definitely for you.
Can I play it with friends?
Yes, the mod has multiplayer support. You can play it on your own server or just together with your friends.
Will there be future updates?
I really want to continue developing the mod, but right now my life is pretty busy with daily life, school, and extremely important exams that will shape my future. Development is basically paused for now. Once things calm down, I plan to pick it back up.
How do I report a bug?
The easiest way is through GitHub. Head over to the repository on my profile, go to the “Issues” tab, and write a detailed description. I'll check it out as soon as I can.
What license is Arda's Sculks released under?
The mod is licensed under the GNU Lesser General Public License Version 2.1 (LGPL-2.1).
