Hey there! I'm Ixra (also known as IxraPlayer), a 17-year-old independent Minecraft mod developer from Istanbul, Turkey.

I specialize in creating unique and immersive mods for both Forge and NeoForge. My journey started with a passion for expanding Minecraft's boundaries, and it led to the creation of my most popular project: Arda's Sculks.

Arda's Sculks is a content-rich mod that adds new sculk-related blocks, items, and mechanics to the deep dark. It's my pride and joy, and seeing the community's response has been incredible. Beyond that, I've released over 10 other mods, ranging from new ores and tools to backpacks and golem variants.

I'm always working on something new, so feel free to check out my projects on Modrinth and CurseForge, or connect with me on social media.
