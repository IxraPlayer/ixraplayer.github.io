Hey! I'm Ixra (IxraPlayer / Ixranium), a Turkish Minecraft mod developer living in Istanbul. I've been playing Minecraft since I was 6 years old — it's more than a game to me, it's the universe where I can turn my imagination into reality.

Over the years I haven't just played; I've built massive structures, drawn textures and pixel art, and developed mods that change how the game feels. I'm an ordinary high school student who loves creating, expanding, and always adding something new on top of what already exists. When I truly have the passion for an idea, I commit to it and deliver quality — I usually work alone and I'm not open to direct requests, because I believe creative freedom brings the best results.

Why I make mods: Whenever I watch a movie, play a game, or read a book, I can't stop imagining different scenarios, alternate fates, and possibilities that never made it into the original. I constantly want to add those visions. Minecraft is the only place where I can actually build them and share them with the world.

My most popular project is Arda's Sculks, a deep dark expansion that adds new blocks, mechanics, and a boss. But I've released over 10 mods for Forge and NeoForge, ranging from ores and tools to golems and psychological horror.

If you enjoy my work, check out my Projects tab or find me on the links — I'm always working on something new.

— Ixra (Turkish Minecraft Modder)
